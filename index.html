Ongoing Research

Anthropomorphic AI Design & User Dependency: A Systematic Mapping Study

Analysis of articles (2018-2026) that explore how humanoid design elements in conversational AI systems —personality, voice, avatar, empathy— influence user behavior, trust, and dependence.

Corpus: 580 articles Sources: IEEE · ACM · ScienceDirect Collaboration: CIMAT · UV Status: Manuscript in preparation
Key finding: 94.8% of studies reporting negative user effects include design mitigation guidelines — suggesting a reactive pattern in HCI research not previously documented at this scale.
